## üìò Usage

1. **Upload Your Document**
   - Simply drag and drop your PDF file into the upload area

2. **Generate Summary**
   - Summafy's AI  will process your document and generate a comprehensive summary

3. **Explore & Interact**
   - Navigate through key points
   - Drill down for additional details
   - Toggle visualization options

4. **Export **
   - Download your summary as PDF or Markdown
